Genki Study Resources

Fork Me

The exercises provided here are for use with Genki: An Integrated Course in Elementary Japanese textbooks (Second Edition) and are meant to help you practice what you have learned in each lesson. To begin studying, choose a lesson from the quick navigation and then the exercise you want to practice for that lesson. If you have any feedback regarding an exercise you can let us know here. Have fun!

For more self-study resources please visit the official self-study room for Genki.

Note: Lessons 0-12 follow the first volume of Genki, listed as "Genki I" below. Lessons 13-23 follow the second volume of Genki, listed as "Genki II" below. This is why the page number references reset after lesson 12, so please make sure to pay attention to the volume used for each lesson.
