Genki Study Resources is a free resource created with the primary goal of making self-study with the Genki textbooks easier. It was developed over the span of 2 years by me, Seth Clydesdale, along with the help of other people both on Github and outside of it. As an open source developer, my primary source of income is donations. So, if you found this project helpful and would like to donate, please see below for ways that you can help support my work.

  • Buy me a coffee or two via Paypal.
  • Buy me a gift from my Amazon Wishlist.

Donations don't always have to be about money, you can also support my work in other ways.

  • Star the project on Github; Stars show appreciation for a repository maintainer's work.
  • Spread the word about Genki Study Resources; let people know how it helped you or how it can help them!
  • Feel free to contact me via one of the platforms located under "Contact info" to say thanks.

Whatever method you choose, I greatly appreciate your support, as it helps motivate me to continue working on and improving my projects. Thank you!
